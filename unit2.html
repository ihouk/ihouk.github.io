Soft & Sweet Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • or 355 g.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter (room temp)
    • 1 1/2 sticks or 170 g.
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
    • or 150 g.
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
    • or 150 g.
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup semisweet/bittersweet chocolate chips
    • or 226 g.

Process:

  1. Adjust oven rack to the middle of the oven. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line three sheet pans with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle, beat the butter on medium speed until creamy, about 1 minute. Add the granulated and brown sugars and beat on medium speed until light and fluffy, 2-3 minutes. Scrape down the sides of the bowl, add the eggs and vanilla, and mix until smooth. Add the flour mixture and beat on low speed until just combined. Add the chocolate and mix into the batter on low speed.
  4. Form the dough into 1 1/2 oz (45 g.) balls (2 tablespoons) and place 8 cookies on each sheet pan.
  5. Bake one pan at a time, rotating halfway through baking. Bake for 8 minutes, until the cookies are slightly puffed in teh center. Bake the cookies until the edges are just turning golden brown but the center is still soft, 2-3 minutes more (10-11 minutes total).
  6. When done baking, transfer the pan of cookies to a wire rack to cool. Let the cookies cool completely before removing from the pan.

Note:

Using chocolate chips instead of chopped chocolate will make for a puffier cookie.
